Texas Caviar

Description

A hearty recipe that's easy to prepare and served as an appetizer or even a topping for chips and cheese

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1 green pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 green onions
  • 2 cans (14 ounces) black eyed peas
  • 1 can (14 ounces) hominy
  • 1 bottle (8 ounces) Italian dressing
  • 1 chopped jalapeno pepper
  • 3 to 4 bags of blue corn chips

Steps

  1. Chop the onion, green pepper, green onion, and jalapeno pepper.
  2. Discard the jalapeno pepper seeds or leave some in for an extra kick.
  3. Mince the garlic.
  4. Drain the chopped tomatoes and the cans of black eyed peas and hominy.
  5. Combine all ingredients except the corn chips in a large bowl.
  6. Let chill for about 30 minutes.
  7. Serve with spoon and corn chips on the side.
