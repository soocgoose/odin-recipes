Chicken Salad

Description

A refreshing dish that's enjoyable as a sandwich or on a bed of lettuce. Great for picnics and easy to prepare and serve.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 3/4 cup celery
  • 1 small green onion
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup coconut
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 3/4 cup red grapes
  • 1/2 package silvered almonds
  • 1 can mandarin oranges
  • romaine lettuce
  • bread (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken breasts in water and dice once fully cooked.
  2. Dice the green onion.
  3. Cut the grapes in half.
  4. Finely chop the celery.
  5. Drain the oranges.
  6. Combine all ingredient in a large bowl.
  7. Let chill for about 30 minutes.
  8. Serve over a bed of lettuce or between slices of bread.
